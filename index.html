MYSTERY TOSSER

How To Play?

Mystery Tosser is indeed a mysterious game. Even I dont know how to win haha!!

You are to create a coins collection by clicking the Make Coins button bellow and to roll your coins, click the Roll Coins button

You can remove any coin from the collection by clicking on it, but this will update the winning criteria

How To Win?

The collection contains some number of green and red coins

To win, you are to toss all the coins in the collection then the mystery happens.

The number of greeen coins after tossing must match with the winning number to win the game.
There is a special reward if you have a 10 point score. STAY MYSTERIOUS, HAHAHA!!!
